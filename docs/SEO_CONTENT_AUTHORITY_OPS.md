# SEO Content & Authority Ops

## Topic Architecture
- Pillar: `Iran Readiness Audit`
- Clusters:
  - Performance & CWV
  - Technical SEO
  - Security & SSRF/Headers
  - Funnel Conversion & Payments

## Content Brief Template (Required Fields)
- Primary intent (`informational` / `commercial`)
- Target query family
- H1 + H2 map
- Required internal links:
  - 1 لینک به `/audit` یا `/en/audit`
  - 1 لینک به pillar
  - 2 لینک به guides مرتبط
- Schema type (`Article`, `BreadcrumbList`)
- CTR meta draft (title + description)
- Update owner + next refresh trigger

## Refresh Policy
- هر guide وقتی یکی از موارد زیر رخ داد refresh می‌شود:
  - تغییر زیرساخت/feature مرتبط
  - افت CTR یا position در GSC
  - تغییر قوانین فنی (SEO/security/performance)

## Authority Program (Safe)
- Linkable assets:
  - checklists
  - practical templates
  - benchmark summaries
- Outreach categories:
  - engineering communities
  - SEO/marketing publications
  - startup ecosystem newsletters
- Safety rules:
  - no paid/spam links
  - no manipulative anchors
  - relevance-first outreach only

## Operational KPI
- Referring domains (quality-scored)
- Link-to-conversion assist rate
- Cluster coverage score (published vs planned)
